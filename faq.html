FAQ

Here you will find to most frequently asked questions.

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

when printing, select HZ printer. Next, walk to the printer, and scan your hz card. Here, you can select what you can print and in what size, after which you can start printing.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Walk to the printer, and scan your hz card. In the menu, click on the option 'scan'. Put the documents you want to scan on the glass plate and press start.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Go to: https://webshop.hz.nl/webshopapp/defaulten.aspx?menu=082076044027019251066025111065201099237062130097. Here you can buy merchandise and other products after logging in with your hz account.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

go to: https://hzuniversity.topdesk.net/tas/public/ssp/content/detail/service?unid=7e54d40b762c4dc79205e200f0d4d818. Here, you can choose a date and time, and the room.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Free parking is available at the car park at Poelendaelesingel 12 in Middelburg. This is at a few minutes’ walk from HZ. When entering the parking space, hold your student card infront of the scanner, and you can enter. There is also a disabled parking space available right next to the HZ building at Het Groene woud 1-3.
